Chilli Recipe

Description

A tasty chilli recipe i've worked on to suit my girlfriend's taste buds.

You can omit the beef for a vegan recipe instead.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 500g of minced beef
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes with juice
  • 1/2 cup of beef stock
  • 4 tablespoons of Mccormick mexican chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoons of vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of salt, add more for your taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat one tablespoon of butter in a soup pot over medium heat. Cook and stir minced beef in the hot oil until no longer pink and the beef is caramelized, about 10 minutes.
  2. Put the beef aside
  3. Melt remaining butter into the soup pot
  4. Add all the dry ingredients
    1. Insert onions and garlic together until caramelized
    2. Insert the chilli powder
    3. Insert the minced beef that you put aside earlier
  5. Insert the remaining ingredients
  6. Cook for about 3 hours
